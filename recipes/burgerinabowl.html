Burger In A Bowl

This tasty burger in a bowl is a great way to get some protein in your diet without sacrificing any of the delicious flavors of fast food.

The total macros will be about 500 calories and 55-60 grams of protein.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound of Grass-Fed Ground Beef
  • 1/4 cup Shredded Cheese
  • 1 Slice of Turkey Bacon
  • 1/4 Onion
  • 1/4 Avocado
  • Pickles
  • Avocado/Olive Oil Spray
  • Seasonings of Choice

Steps

  1. Dice up your onion, pickles, and avocado.
  2. Season your ground beef and separate it into two equal size patties.
  3. Warm your pan up on medium-high heat.
  4. Spray some oil onto the pan.
  5. Place the burgers on the pan, making sure to get a good sear on the meat. That is how you get the extra umami flavor.
  6. Flip the burgers, then place equal parts cheese on each bun.
  7. Add a lid on top so the cheese melts.
  8. Once the patties are cookes, take them off, put them in a bowl, and oil the pan again.
  9. Place the diced onions on one side, letting them caramelize.
  10. Place the slice of turkey bacon on the other side.
  11. Once the onions are cooked, throw them into the bowl.
  12. Place the bacon on a cutting board and cut it into small pieces.
  13. Add the bacon, pickles, and avocado to the bowl.
  14. Top with a sauce of your choice and enjoy!
